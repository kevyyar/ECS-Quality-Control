# Keep Submitted and Closed Records Immutable

Submitted Inspections and Closed Tickets are proof records, so they are not edited after submission or closure. If a mistake needs to be explained later, the app uses an additive Correction Note instead of changing the original inspection answers, photos, or ticket closure evidence.

## Considered Options

- Allow edits to submitted inspections and closed tickets, which makes typo/photo corrections easy but weakens report trust.
- Keep submitted and closed records immutable, which requires additive corrections but preserves accountability.

## Consequences

Draft Inspections remain editable. Once submitted, inspection findings and failure evidence become historical records; once closed, ticket closure evidence becomes part of the proof record.
